The Wind Turbine
Interactive Educational App

This project began with the challenge of building a 3D model of a structurally accurate, modern day Wind Turbine. Once I built the turbine, I planned to build the web application with the vision of it being stationed as an interactive, educational museum kiosk. The Wind turbine takes up the entirety of the web page as it animates, and the user is immersed as they explore the different parts and pieces of the Wind Turbine's structure and its historical timeline.
