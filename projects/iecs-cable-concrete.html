IECS Cable Concrete
Animated Web Infographic

Over the summer of 2019, Aaron Patterson and I were hired to build a client-based project through Fanshawe College. The goal was to visualize the environmental and economical value of using IECS' cable concrete compared to conventional rip-rap. We approached the design as a visually exciting web infographic with a linear story. We wanted it to be accesible to customers and IECS employees as a selling tool. Design assets were created by Aaron Patterson in Adobe Illustrator. Together we both built the story, structure, animation and interactivity from the ground up.
