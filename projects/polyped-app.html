Project Goal

This application is designed to be used as an interactive, educational application. It was created and released under my custom, imaginary edutainment brand called "Polyped". The goal of the project was to design an effective method to communicate the unique story and qualities of the Ant-mimicking Spider. 3D models and assets were painted within Cinema 4D and exported for use in this custom designed and built web application.

Design

From the beginning of the user experience, the user plays a mini-game that challenges them to find out which "ant" is not like the others. This engages their curiosity and encourages them to analyze the Ant-mimicking spider on their own.

Once they choose the correct option, they are introduced to the main aplication where interesting information about the Ant-mimicking is interactively organized and structured.

Narrative

Research

Extensive online research and data collection about the Ant-mimicking spider was carried out in order to construct a storyline for the application. The research was then reduced to the most compelling details and arranged into a cohesive, linear fashion that would carry out the application's storyline.
