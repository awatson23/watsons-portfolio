Ant-mimicking Spider Interactive Application

This application is designed to be used as an interactive, educational application. It was created and released under my custom, imaginary edutainment brand called "Polyped". The goal of the project was to plan and execute an effective user experience design to communicate the unique story of the Ant-mimicking spider. The user is sent on a quest of discovery. One of these bugs is not like the others. Can you spot the imposter bug? The Ant-mimicking spider is revealed and the user continues to learn what makes the Ant-mimicking spider different from the rest. 3D models were painted and exported through Cinema 4D.
